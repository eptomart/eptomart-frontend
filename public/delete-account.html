Delete Your Account

We respect your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of your Eptomart account at any time using either method below.

Option 1 — Delete via the App

Open the Eptomart app → Go to Profile → Scroll down → Tap Delete Account. Your account and data will be permanently deleted.

Option 2 — Request via Email

Send an email to eptosicare@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the mobile number or email address linked to your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile, name, email, and phone number
  • Your saved addresses
  • Your cart and wishlist
  • Your account login credentials

What we retain

  • Order and payment records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by Indian tax law (GST compliance).

Contact

For any questions about your data, contact us at eptosicare@gmail.com.
